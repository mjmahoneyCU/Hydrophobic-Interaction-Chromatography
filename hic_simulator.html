Bed is packed and equilibrated in 2 M ammonium sulfate. Load the sample to begin.

How it works. Proteins carry hydrophobic patches on their surface. A strong salting-out salt like ammonium sulfate orders the surrounding water, making it costly to keep those patches wetted — so they tuck against the resin's hydrophobic ligands. High salt = bound.
